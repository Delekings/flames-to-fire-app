Scripture
"For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."
Jeremiah 29:11 · NIV · Change translation
Reflection
God's timing is rarely our timing. We live in an age of instant gratification, where waiting feels like failure. But the scriptures remind us that God works all things together — not some things, not easy things — all things, for the good of those who love Him.

Abraham waited 25 years for the promised son. Joseph spent 13 years in slavery and prison before his elevation. Yet in each case, the waiting was not wasted — it was preparation. God was not slow; He was thorough.
Prayer
Lord, I surrender my timeline to You today. Where I have been anxious and impatient, forgive me. Teach me to trust that Your delays are not denials, and that everything You have promised will come to pass in Your perfect time. Amen.
