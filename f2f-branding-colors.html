Official Color System

🔥
Primary
Flame Orange
#E8500A
Bold, passionate energy. The fire at the heart of the brand — used for CTAs, key highlights, and the iconic flame mark.
Accent
Ember Gold
#F5A623
Warmth and illumination. Reflects glory and light — used for headings, icons, and supporting accent elements.
Secondary
Sacred Green
#4A8C2A
Growth, renewal, and life. Directly drawn from the logo — used for headers, section dividers, and brand consistency.
Deep
Forest Deep
#2D5A1B
Depth and rootedness. Used for backgrounds, footers, and areas requiring gravitas and weight.
Neutral
Smoke Charcoal
#2B2B2B
Authority and strength. Primary dark background — used for body text and dark-mode surfaces.
Light
Ash White
#F7F4EE
Clean, warm purity. A warm off-white for light backgrounds, giving a soft, sacred feel over stark white.

Color Usage Guide

Primary Action
Flame Orange
Buttons, links, fire icon, key callouts, donation CTAs, newsletter sign-ups. The most attention-demanding color — use sparingly and intentionally.
Highlights & Headings
Ember Gold
Section titles, pull quotes, scripture references, badge backgrounds. Pairs brilliantly with both the deep green and charcoal.
Brand Foundation
Sacred Green
Logo, headers, nav bars, brand banners, social media frames. The identity anchor that connects all materials to the logo.
Backgrounds & Text
Ash White + Charcoal
Use Ash White for light-mode backgrounds and body text areas. Charcoal for dark surfaces and text on light backgrounds.
